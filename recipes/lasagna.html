Lasagna

Description

When my grandmother fled impoverished Italia, she carried this recipe hidden in her brassier so that the yuppie border control would not learn her secrets.

Now, for the first time in history, I am sharing this recipe with the general public. My nonna will be rolling in her grave, but I feel it must be done for the good of humanity.

This recipe is so spectacular that when I personally served it to the heads of the US and USSR in the 1980s, they bonded over their shared love of it and thus ended the cold war.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Beef mince
  • Lasagna sauce

Steps

  1. Layer lasagna sheets, beef mince and lasagna sauce in a deep oven dish.
  2. Put in oven and cook for half an hour
  3. Take out and eat, yum!
